📖 Meaning & Importance | Maana na Umuhimu
🔢 Significant figures (Tarakimu Muhimu) reflect the precision of a measurement. All non-zero digits are significant; captive zeros are significant; leading zeros are never significant; trailing zeros are significant only if a decimal point is present.
📌 Example: 0.00520 → 3 sig figs (5,2,0).
📐 Tarakimu Muhimu zinaonyesha usahihi wa kipimo. Tarakimu zote zisizo sifuri ni muhimu; sifuri zilizozingirwa kati ya tarakimu muhimu ni muhimu; sifuri mwanzoni hazina maana; sifuri za mwisho ni muhimu tu ikiwa kuna nukta desimali.
📌 Mfano: 0.00520 → tarakimu muhimu 3 (5,2,0).
